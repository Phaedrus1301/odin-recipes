Grilled-Asparagus

Description

The best grilled asparagus becomes smoky and sweetly caramelized when the grill does its job it does so well, when the outside of these bright green spears become lightly charred and crisped, and the interior soft and tender. Behold, these are the simple steps for making this vegetable side dish creation the easy way.

Ingridients

  • Olive oil
  • salt
  • lemon
  • black pepper

Steps

  1. Step 1- Drizzle with olive oil and toss
  2. Step 2- Season with salt and pepper and toss again
  3. Step 3- Cook asparagus on grill for 5 mins and its done
